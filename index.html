Standard operating procedure for threat mitigation

2

Report & Block

Threat Intelligence

Latest cybersecurity advisories and awareness tips

APK Frauds

Never install apps from unverified links sent via WhatsApp or SMS. They often contain malware to steal OTPs.

UPI Request Scams

You only need to enter your UPI PIN to SEND money, never to RECEIVE money. Ignore "Click to receive" links.

Part-time Job Traps

Beware of WhatsApp messages offering easy money for liking YouTube videos or reviewing hotels. They are Ponzi schemes.

Emergency Response

Have you already lost money to a cyber fraud? Time is critical.

  • Call 1930 immediately to freeze the transaction
  • Do NOT delete the chat, SMS, or transaction history
  • Gather screenshots of profiles and messages
